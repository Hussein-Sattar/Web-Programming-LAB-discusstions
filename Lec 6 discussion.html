1. What are the differences between the opacity property and alpha channel in rgba or hsla?

        Opacity Property: Affects the entire element and its content with values from 0 (transparent) to 1 (opaque).
         Inherited by child elements.
        Alpha Channel (RGBA/HSLA): Controls the transparency of a specific color without affecting the entire element.
         Values range from 0 (transparent) to 1 (opaque). Not inherited by child elements.
